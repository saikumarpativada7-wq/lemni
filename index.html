User Guide

"Welcome to Lemni. This is not just a quiz; it is a structured training program designed to build a bulletproof numerical foundation."

1. Getting Started

Your dashboard is the central hub. Here is what you need to know:

  • Mode Switcher: Toggle between Pro (Full features) and Kids (Simplified, safe mode with balloons 🎈).
  • Start Practice: The main training ground. Take timed quizzes to build speed.
  • Study Zone: Your reference library. Memorize essential numbers before testing yourself.
  • Progress: Your personal report card. Visit daily to see your "Coach's Tip".

2. How to Use "Practice Mode"

This is where the work happens. Follow these steps:

  • Select Operation: Choose a specific skill (e.g., Addition, Numerical Ability).
  • Select Difficulty:
    • • Beginner: Simple 1-2 digit numbers.
    • • Intermediate: Larger numbers, introducing carry-over.
    • • Expert/Master: Complex calculations for advanced users.
  • Select Questions:
    • • Fixed Sets (10-50): Stats are SAVED. Use these to earn badges.
    • • Adaptive Mode (Infinity): Stats are NOT SAVED. Difficulty increases when you are right and decreases when you are wrong. Use this for casual "flow state" training.

Inside the Game Session

  • The Timer: Competitive exams require answering in under 15-30 seconds.
  • Feedback: The screen flashes Green for correct and Red for incorrect.
  • Results Screen: Click the "Solution" button next to any wrong answer to see a step-by-step breakdown.

3. The Study Zone

You cannot calculate everything; some things must be memorized.

  • Learn Tab: View reference lists (e.g., Squares up to 30).
  • Practice Tab: Rapid-fire drills to test recall. Note: Drill stats are NOT recorded.

4. The AI Coach & Mastery

The Progress screen is powered by a smart coaching engine designed to guide you through a logical skill tree.

🏆 Mastery Badges

When you perform well on a specific stage (50+ questions total, 100% Accuracy for early stages or >90% for later stages, Fast Time), you earn a Mastery Badge. This badge acts as a "shield," preventing the Coach from nagging you about that topic for 30 days.

🎯 Priority Focus Areas

Instead of a random list of mistakes, the app calculates your "Weakest Link":

  • Prerequisites First: If you fail an advanced topic, the Coach checks if your fundamentals are solid first.
  • Accuracy > Speed: The Coach won't nag you about speed until your accuracy is above target.

5. Recommended Daily Routine

The 15-Minute Loop:

  1. Check Progress: Look at your "Priority Focus Area." That is your mission.
  2. Warm Up: Spend 2 minutes in the Study Zone drilling specific tables.
  3. Execute: Do a set of 20 questions in Practice Mode at the recommended difficulty.
  4. Review: Hit 90% accuracy? Check the Coach for your next target.

"The AI Coach uses logic rules to analyze your performance. While designed to be helpful, it may behave differently in edge cases or give advice that doesn't match your personal learning style.

Please do not take it too seriously. You are the ultimate judge of your own progress. If you disagree with the Coach, feel free to ignore the tips and practice what you feel is best."

"Amateurs practice until they get it right. Professionals practice until they can't get it wrong."
